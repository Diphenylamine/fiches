Tsugumi Oba

Tsugumi Ooba is a Japanese writer and screenwriter. He was born in Tokyo. He is the author of the famous manga Death Notebook. Oba's real name is unknown. Some fans think that Tsugumi Oba is just a pseudonym for Hiroshi Gamo. As stated in the profile, when work on the next installment of the Death Note manga begins, Oba collects cups and concocts the plot while holding his knees in his chair, like L, one of the main characters in the series. Characters
Light Yagami

The protagonist of the manga, anime series and Death Note films. In the story, Light is shown as a highly intelligent, calculating young man with a heightened sense of justice. As the series progresses, Light is shown to be in possession of the Notebook of Death, a notebook which can be used to kill a man with impunity once his name and face are written down. As the story progresses, he becomes a rather arrogant and condescending character, thinking himself to be the god of the new world. With the help of the Death Notebook, Yagami Light decided to create a new world without criminals and began punishing anyone who committed crimes. But later, when Detective L cornered him, he began gradually getting rid of anyone who sought him out or stood in his way.

Misa Amane

The heroine of the manga, anime series and Death Notebook films. Famous photo model who won Readers' Choice magazine's "18" contest. Based on the film, a popular TV presenter. Few would have recognized her as the murderer who caused a stir in the Sakura TV studio, the second Kira. Grateful to Kira for punishing her parents' killer, she is willing to do whatever it takes to find him. Falling in love with Yagami Light, she makes a two-time deal with the God of Death to gain the Eyes of the God of Death and be of service to him. Often refers to herself in the third person, referring to herself as "Misa-Misa".

Ryuk

One of the main characters from the manga, anime, films and computer games Death Notebook. The concept and image was invented by Tsugumi Oba and the character's appearance is by Takeshi Obata. Ryuk is one of the Death Gods, a supernatural creatures who need to kill mortals in order to prolong their lives by using the Death Notebook, a mystical artifact that kills a person after writing their name in it.

L

One of the main characters from the manga, anime series and Death Notebook movies. In the story, he is regarded as the best detective in the world and confronts serial killer Kira, who is in possession of the Death Notebook. He successfully hid his name, identity, and face even from Interpol, but in the process of investigating Kira, revealed his identity to the most trustworthy police officers and two suspects. With everyone else, he communicated by hiding his face behind the letter L, written in Old London script. The only confidant known to the audience by the beginning of the series is Watari. In addition to his alias "L", the character uses two other aliases, "Erald Coyle" , who are known as other prominent detectives. Before the investigation team and Yagami Light, he asks to call himself Ryuzaki , which he says is for conspiracy purposes. His real name, according to the manga, is Al Lawlai.
